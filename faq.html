Getting Started

To start trading in the stock market:
  1. Open a Demat and Trading account with a registered broker
  2. Complete your KYC verification
  3. Link your bank account
  4. Study market basics and trading strategies
  5. Start with small investments to gain experience

Required documents include:
  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account Statement/Passbook
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Income Proof (optional)

Trading Basics

Intraday Trading: Buying and selling stocks within the same trading day. Positions must be closed before market closes.

Delivery Trading: Buying stocks and holding them for multiple days. Stocks are transferred to your Demat account.

Stock charts show price movements over time. Key elements include:

  • Candlestick patterns showing price open, high, low, and close
  • Moving averages for trend identification
  • Volume indicators for trading activity
  • Technical indicators like RSI, MACD for market analysis

Platform Usage

To use TradingView charts:

  1. Select your preferred timeframe (1D, 1W, 1M, etc.)
  2. Choose chart type (Candlestick, Line, Bar)
  3. Use drawing tools for technical analysis
  4. Add indicators from the toolbar
  5. Save your chart layouts for future use

To set price alerts:

  1. Go to the Price Alerts section
  2. Select your target stock
  3. Set your desired price level
  4. Choose notification method (email/SMS)
  5. Save your alert settings
